STEELFOAM®

SteelFoam FANOSA® is an innovative, sustainable and high-performance construction system that maximizes energy efficiency for the entire life of buildings. It is made to measure, with durable, resistant and recyclable materials, ideal for handling in tall buildings.

It is made up of posts made of galvanized sheet in different gauges and self-extinguishing Expanded Polystyrene (EPS). Used for both curtain and / or plug type facade walls, as well as partition walls, parapets, volumes or architectural forms.
