CONSTRUPANEL®

FANOSA® Construpanel is an ideal construction system for modern buildings; it has been used worldwide for more than 30 years. It is made up of self-extinguishing Expanded Polystyrene plates of different thicknesses and densities. On both sides, it has a steel mesh with cross-connectors welded together. A versatile and light product, resistant to wind and earthquake loads, it is quick to install, economical, and allows the selection of wide variety of finishes.
