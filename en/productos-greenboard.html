Greenboard

The FANOSA® Greenboard insulation board is a high quality product that offers greater thermal resistance for a lower price within the construction industry. Its marks on the surface promote a better anchorage of the coatings and is totally safe due to its self-extinguishing additive.

For its installation, it is highly recommended to use our comprehensive Aislaterm® system, which guarantees optimal anchorage and covering. Learn more about the Aislaterm® system here. (Include link to consult the Aislaterm® page.)
