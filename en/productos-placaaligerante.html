Lightening Board

The FANOSA® Lightweight Plate is the ideal insulation and light solution for the construction of waffle slabs. Due to the characteristics of the system in which it is used and the benefits of its design, lightening and insulation are obtained in one step.

Its use is focused on the construction of slabs, either mezzanine or roof, mainly in residential homes, without leaving the option of being able to solve slabs of greater gaps; such as commercial premises, offices, small warehouses and extensions.
