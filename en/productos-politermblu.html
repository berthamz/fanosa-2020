Politerm® Blu

Politerm® Blu is the ideal construction additive to create lightweight mortars with low thermal conductivity. It is made up of light Expanded Polystyrene Beads integrated with the additive E.I.A.®, which provides excellent adhesion to cement and eliminates the flotation phenomenon of common polystyrene beads, guaranteeing a homogeneous distribution throughout the mix. Pearls are neither toxic nor absorbent, on the contrary, they are resistant and dimensionally stable over time.

Versions:
  • Politerm® Blu= 3-6 mm diameter.
  • Politerm® Blu Fein= 2.5 mm. Diameter.

ADVANTAGES

Politerm® Blu tiene muchas bondades, estas son algunas de las principales ventajas de este sistema constructivo

  • Perfect mix

  • Homogeneous distribution

  • Excellent pumping capacity

  • Prevents flotation of Styrofoam Beads

  • Mortars with better thermal resistance

  • Weight reduction in buildings
