Insulating Board

FANOSA® Insulating Board is a product widely used in the construction industry for the thermal coating of walls, ceiling slabs and floors of cold rooms, buildings, industrial buildings, commercial premises and residential houses. This thanks to its great resistance to heat, lightness and low cost of acquisition and installation.
