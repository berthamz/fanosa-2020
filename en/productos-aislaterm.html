Aislaterm®

Aislaterm® is the FANOSA® line that has everything you need to cover all our Expanded Polystyrene (EPS) products. With Aislaterm®, highly efficient constructions are obtained throughout the useful life of the building or house, minimizing greenhouse gas emissions and achieving high savings in energy expenditure without neglecting the final appearance of your project.

Its components are the adhesive and Base Coat coating and Fiberglass Reinforcement Mesh.

Aislaterm® is the ideal recommended coating for our Steelfoam® products and all our insulation boards. Reach out to the closest representative in your area and ask for more information.
