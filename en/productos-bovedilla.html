Classic Filler Block

FANOSA® Filler Block is the support and lightening element used for joist and filler block construction systems. It is a specially designed block of expanded polystyrene (EPS) that is cut, shaped and dimensioned for use with a wide variety of joists for the construction of concrete slabs working in one direction. The result is a system with excellent structural, thermal and acoustic properties. It is ideal for building structural slabs for housing developments, residential work, industrial buildings, etc.
